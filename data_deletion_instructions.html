Data Deletion Instructions

If you wish to delete your data, please follow the steps below:

Step 1: Send an Email Request

Send an email to your-email@example.com with the subject line "Data Deletion Request". Please include your username and any relevant details about your account.

Step 2: Confirmation

Once we receive your request, we will verify your identity and process your data deletion request. You will receive a confirmation email once your data has been deleted.

Contact Us

If you have any questions about this process, please contact us at your-email@example.com.
